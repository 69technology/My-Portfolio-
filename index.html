04 / My Process

How I turn ideas into
real experiences.

Step 01

Discover

Deep user research, stakeholder interviews, competitive analysis, and empathy mapping to understand the real problem before touching a tool.

Step 02

Define

Synthesizing research into clear problem statements, user personas, and journey maps that align design goals with business outcomes.

Step 03

Design

Wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, interactive prototypes, and design systems built in Figma — tested with real users through iterative cycles.

Step 04

Deliver

Clean, accessible, production-ready code. Handoff documentation, developer collaboration, and post-launch iteration to ensure the vision ships.
